Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 18, 2026

1. Overview

Click and Clear is a Chrome extension designed to help users delete their browsing data with a single click or keyboard shortcut. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Key Statement: Click and Clear does NOT collect, track, or store any of your personal information, browsing history, or user activity. All operations are performed locally on your device.

2. What Data We Collect

Data We Collect:

  • User preferences for deletion settings (which data types to delete)
  • User's preferred time range for data deletion

Data We DO NOT Collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, address, phone number)
  • Your browsing history or visited websites
  • Cookies or tracking data
  • Financial or payment information
  • Location information or IP addresses
  • Health or medical information
  • Passwords or authentication credentials
  • Keyboard input or mouse movements
  • Website content or files
  • Any analytics or usage data

3. How We Use Your Data

The limited data we store (your preferences) is used only to:

  • Remember your deletion preferences between sessions
  • Remember your preferred time range selection
  • Enable a consistent user experience

We never:

  • Share your preferences with third parties
  • Transmit data to external servers
  • Use your data for analytics or marketing
  • Combine your data with other services
  • Sell or monetize your information

4. Where Data Is Stored

Local Storage Only

All data is stored exclusively in chrome.storage.local, which means:

  • Your preferences are stored only on your device
  • Data is not synced to Chrome cloud or any server
  • Data remains private and under your complete control
  • Uninstalling the extension deletes all stored data

5. Permissions Explained

browsingData Permission

This permission allows the extension to delete browsing data when you request it. We:

  • Only delete data when you explicitly click the delete button or use the keyboard shortcut
  • Do not read or access your browsing data
  • Do not transmit any data externally

commands Permission

This permission enables keyboard shortcuts (Ctrl+Shift+D on Windows/Linux, Cmd+Shift+D on Mac). We:

  • Only register system-level keyboard events
  • Do not monitor keyboard input for any other purpose
  • Do not track keystroke patterns

tabs Permission

This permission allows closing open browser tabs when you select that option. We:

  • Only close tabs when you explicitly enable and request this feature
  • Do not read tab content or history
  • Do not monitor tab activity

storage Permission

This permission allows storing your preferences. We:

  • Store only your deletion preferences and time range
  • Use only local storage (not cloud sync)
  • Never transmit stored data externally

6. User Control

You have complete control over the extension:

  • Choose what to delete: Select checkboxes for each data type
  • Choose when to delete: Manually trigger deletion or use keyboard shortcuts
  • Clear preferences: Remove all stored data through Chrome settings
  • Uninstall anytime: All data is deleted when you remove the extension

7. Third-Party Services

Click and Clear does not use any third-party services, analytics providers, or external APIs. The extension operates entirely within your browser with no external connections.

8. Data Deletion Capabilities

When you use Click and Clear, you can delete:

  • Browsing history
  • Cookies and site data
  • Cached images and files
  • Download history
  • Autofill form data
  • Passwords (if selected)
  • Open tabs and windows (if selected)
  • Service workers
  • IndexedDB and Web SQL data

9. Security

Your data security is important to us:

  • No external server connections
  • No data transmission over the internet
  • All operations are local to your device
  • Uses Chrome's native deletion APIs
  • No intermediate data storage

10. Children's Privacy

Click and Clear does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The extension does not target children and contains no age-restricted content. Parents concerned about their child's use should monitor their Chrome extension installations.

11. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the extension constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact & Support

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our practices, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store extension page.

Summary: We collect minimal data, store it only locally on your device, never share it with anyone, and you have complete control over all settings and data deletion.
